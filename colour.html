Rationale

7th Hells colour palette is inspired by the rich and vibrant colours, to the dull and eery hues found in the settings of the zombie apololiptic school of 7th Hell. The primary colors are bold and intense, reflecting the fiery and chaotic nature of the game's setting. The secondary colors provide a complementary contrast, adding depth and variety to the overall design. Each color has been carefully selected to evoke specific emotions and enhance the visual storytelling of the game, creating an immersive experience for players. Continue to use these colors consistently across all branding and design materials to maintain a cohesive and recognizable visual identity for 7th Hell. But, what makes a big part of the game's visual identity is the use of dark and muted colours, which are used to create a sense of tension and unease, and to reflect the game's themes of horror and survival. As you go deeper into the depths of the school, deceding into the lower levels, the colours become more muted and desaturated, reflecting the increasing danger and despair of the environment. Along the dark and muted colours, there are also some brighter and more vibrant colours used in the game, such as the red of blood and the green of toxic zombies, which are used to create a sense of contrast and to draw attention to important elements in the game. This creates a balanced and dynamic visual experience for players, and helps to reinforce the game's themes and atmosphere.
